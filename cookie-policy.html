No-Cookie Policy

{Last Updated: 10 January 2025}



0. Welcome to GENiALABILE

GENiALABILE do not uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience, analyze website performance, and deliver personalized content. This No-Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, why we do not use them, and your options for managing cookies in other website.


1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites you visit. They serve a variety of purposes, such as remembering your preferences, enabling functionality, and collecting data for analytics.


2. Types of Cookies We Do Not Use

[·] Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our website. These cookies cannot be disabled.
[·] Analytics Cookies: Used to gather data on how users interact with other website, including page visits and time spent on pages. We do not use Google Analytics for this purpose.
[·] Marketing Cookies: Help deliver relevant advertisements based on your browsing behavior.


3. Why We Do Not Use Cookies

[·] We do not need to enhance website functionality and provide a seamless user experience.
[·] We do not need to analyze traffic and performance using tools like Google Analytics.
[·] We do not need to display personalized content and targeted advertisements.


4. Managing Your Cookies In Other Website

You have the right to manage your cookie preferences:
[·] Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Refer to your browser's help section for instructions.
[·] Use platforms like Your Online Choices (EU) or YourAdChoices (US) to opt out of targeted advertising.
[·] Disable Google Analytics tracking with the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

If you do not agree with the use of cookies, you may disable them using the dedicated options or exit other website immediately. Continuing to use other website implies consent to our Cookie Policy. In our website this action is not needed, because we do not use cookies.


5. Cookies Do Not Used on This Website

Below is a list of the cookies we do not use:
[·] Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
[·] Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until deleted manually or automatically after a defined period.
[·] Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services like Google Analytics or social media platforms.


6. Data Protection

All data collected via cookies is anonymized wherever possible. Personal data processed through cookies complies with applicable laws, including GDPR (EU) and CCPA (US).


7. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this No-Cookie Policy. Changes will be effective upon posting, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
