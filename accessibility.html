Accessibility Statement

{Last Updated: 09 January 2025}



0. Welcome to GENiALABILE

GENiALABILE is committed to providing a website that is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to ensure our platform meets or exceeds the requirements of applicable laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).


1. Features Supporting Accessibility

We have implemented the following features to enhance accessibility:
[·] Keyboard navigation for users without a mouse.
[·] Screen reader compatibility for visually impaired users.
[·] Text scaling support for users with visual difficulties.
[·] Color contrast adjustments for better readability.


2. Third-Party Tools and Limitations

Some content on this site may rely on third-party integrations, such as Google Analytics. We are working to ensure these services are accessible but cannot guarantee full compliance.


3. Continuous Improvements

GENiALABILE continuously monitors accessibility standards and makes updates where necessary. Your feedback is invaluable to us. If you have suggestions or notice issues, feel free to contact us.


4. How to Request Assistance
